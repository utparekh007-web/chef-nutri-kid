Age Guide
What Your Child Needs at Each Stage 👶
👶
0–2 Years
Focus on iron-rich foods, soft textures. No salt, sugar or honey. Breast milk + soft purees. High calorie density needed for rapid growth.
🧒
2–4 Years
Introduce variety of textures and flavors. Finger foods, dals, roti. Avoid processed foods. 3 meals + 2 snacks per day for energy.
🧑
5–8 Years
School-age nutrition — brain foods like eggs, nuts, fish. Higher calcium for bone growth. Limit junk food and sugary drinks.
👦
9–12 Years
Pre-teen growth spurt — higher protein, iron (especially girls), zinc. Sports nutrition if active. Build healthy eating habits before teen years.
